Hayabusa Nəsilləri

Generation 1 (1999-2007)

The Ultimate Predator

The first generation Suzuki Hayabusa broke all records, instantly becoming the fastest production motorcycle in the world.

  • Engine: 1,299 cc
  • Power: 175 hp
  • Top Speed: 312 km/h (194 mph)

Generation 2 (2008-2020)

Refined Power

The second generation refined the beast, increasing displacement and bringing effective aerodynamic updates.

  • Engine: 1,340 cc
  • Power: 197 hp
  • Top Speed: 299 km/h (186 mph restricted)

Generation 3 (2021-Present)

Modern Tech Meets Heritage

The latest Hayabusa brings modern electronics, ride-by-wire throttle and advanced traction control.

  • Engine: 1,340 cc (Euro 5)
  • Power: 187 hp
  • Top Speed: 299 km/h (186 mph restricted)
